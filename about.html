Our Story

Every year, several buildings are built and several more are demolished. But where does all these debris go? They become waste which in turn spoils the soil and the environment. So, here we have come with something out of the box.

  • Recycle used up concrete and reuse in new constructions.
  • Manufacturing of building materails from recycleable commodities
  • Reducing carbon footprint and fly ash dispersion.
  • Reducing risk of health hazards and accidents.
  • Recycling non-biodegradable wastes.
  • Use of high-tech machinery to save time and resources.
